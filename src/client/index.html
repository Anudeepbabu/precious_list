Simple, Secure, Automatic

Three steps to ensure your family never has to search for what you own.

1

Create Your Catalog

List everything you own: bank accounts, insurance policies, investments, real estate, vehicles, digital subscriptions, business interests. For each asset, record what it is, where it is held, account identifiers, and how to reclaim or close it.

2

Designate Inheritors

Choose who should receive information about your assets. Designate inheritors and trusted contacts. Each person automatically receives a personalized, read-only summary containing only information relevant to them.

3

Auto-Updated Summaries

Your designated contacts receive summaries with reclamation instructions and contact details. When you update your catalog, their summaries automatically update. No manual coordination needed.

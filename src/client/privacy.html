Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy is adapted from the open source privacy policy provided by Automattic under a Creative Commons Sharealike license.

Who We Are and What This Policy Covers

Precious List is designed to help you organize your assets and protect your family's future. Our mission is to bring clarity to the chaos of asset management.

This Privacy Policy applies to information that we collect about you when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We only collect information about you if we have a reason to do so â€” for example, to provide our Services, to communicate with you, or to make our Services better.

  • Basic account information: We ask for basic information from you in order to set up your account. For example, we require individuals who sign up to provide an email address and password.
  • Asset Information: You provide us with information about your assets, such as bank accounts, insurance policies, and property, to organize them within our Service.
  • Contact information: We may collect contact information for your trusted contacts (people you share information with) to facilitate the Service.

How and Why We Use Information

We use information about you for the purposes listed below:

  • To provide our Services: For example, to set up and maintain your account, and to organize your asset information.
  • To ensure quality and improve our Services: For example, by monitoring and analyzing how users interact with our Services so we can create new features.
  • To communicate with you: For example, by emailing you to ask for your feedback or share tips.

Sharing Information

We do not sell your private personal information. We share information about you in the limited circumstances spelled out below and with appropriate safeguards on your privacy:

  • With your consent: We may share and disclose information with your consent or at your direction. For example, we share your asset information with the trusted contacts you designate.
  • Business transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company, user information would likely be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party.
  • Legal Requests: We may disclose information about you in response to a subpoena, court order, or other governmental request.

Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, Precious List may change its Privacy Policy from time to time. Precious List encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your further use of the Services after a change to our Privacy Policy will be subject to the updated policy.
